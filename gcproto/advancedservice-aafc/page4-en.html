4. After you apply

We may contact you for additional information. After you submit your completed application we will:

  • acknowledge receipt of your application within 1 business day;
  • assess your application and send you an approval or rejection letter within 100 business days of receiving a complete application package;
  • send you an agreement, if you have been approved, for signature within 30 business days of receiving the completed documentation required to meet the eligibility criteria;
  • send you a payment within 30 business days of receipt of a duly completed and documented claim. This is applicable only if an agreement is signed.

We aim to work within these timelines, however actual times may vary.

There are currently no delays in processing applications.

Our current application processing time is less than 100 business days.

Forms for Internships

The following forms must be submitted throughout funded internships.

  • Forms for employers:
    • Participant Information Form – to propose projects and identify employees
    • Final Report of the Employer Form – to provide a final report of the internship and assessment of the interns
    • Financial Claim Form – to provide proof of salary payments
    • Financial Claim Form for small non-profit entities
  • Form for interns:
    • Employee's Assessment Report on the Project Form – to provide assessment of the internship and information about themselves

Assessment and approval

Assessment criteria

Departmental Officials will evaluate proposals to determine the merit of projects. If a project is selected, the applicant will be contacted and required to sign a contribution agreement with AAFC.

Contribution agreement

Successful Applicants who enter into a Contribution Agreement with AAFC will be referred to therein as Recipients. Recipients are required to enter into a Contribution Agreement that outlines the amount of funding AAFC will provide to the Recipient toward eligible costs, as well as the Recipient's responsibilities and obligations.

Payments to Recipients

AAFC will make payments based on claims submitted for eligible costs incurred and paid by the Recipient in accordance with the terms and conditions of the Contribution Agreement. Each claim must be supported by details of the costs being claimed and certified by an authorized official of the Recipient.

For projects of less than eight months, AAFC may reimburse up to 100% of the contribution approved in the Contribution Agreement at the end of the internship, upon receipt of the final report, proof of salary payment to the candidate, and a completed Financial Claim Form.

For projects of eight months or longer, AAFC may reimburse up to 50% of the contribution approved in the Contribution Agreement after the first half of the internship, only upon receipt of the completed Financial Claim Form and proof of salary payment to the candidate. The payment of the last 50% is made at the end of the internship, upon receipt of the final report, the Financial Claim Form and proof of salary payment to the candidate.

Agricultural Youth Green Jobs Initiative funds may only be allocated for salaries and social benefits. The employer is responsible for paying the employee.

Reporting requirements

The Employer is responsible for completing and submitting to AAFC the required documents and evaluation reports at the mid-point and/or at the end of the Project and to participate in the initiative evaluation by AAFC which could occur up to 36 months after the end of the initiative.
